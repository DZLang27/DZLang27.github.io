探索中不断前进的Taylor公式

发表于2022-12-04|更新于2022-12-04|math
|字数总计:1.3k|阅读时长:7分钟|阅读量:

引入

故事的开始是下面的式子(微分中值公式):

f(x)−f(x0)=f′(ξ)(x−x0)f\left(x\right)-f\left(x_0\right)=f^\prime\left(\xi\right)\left(x-x_0\right) f(x)−f(x0​)=f′(ξ)(x−x0​)

即

f(x)=f(x0)+f′(ξ)(x−x0)+o(x−x0)=T1(x)+o(x−x0)f\left(x\right)=f\left(x_0\right)+f^\prime\left(\xi\right)\left(x-x_0\right)+o\left(x-x_0\right)=T_1\left(x\right)+o\left(x-x_0\right) f(x)=f(x0​)+f′(ξ)(x−x0​)+o(x−x0​)=T1​(x)+o(x−x0​)

其中

T1(x)=f(x0)+f′(ξ)(x−x0)T_1\left(x\right)=f\left(x_0\right)+f^\prime\left(\xi\right)\left(x-x_0\right) T1​(x)=f(x0​)+f′(ξ)(x−x0​)

且ξ在X与X0之间。
从这当中我发现一个函数在某一点的领域内可以用一个线性函数近似替代。既然如此，那能不能用一个二次来替代呢?即是否存在函数T2(x),使得:

f(x)=T2(x)+o((x−x0)2)f\left(x\right)=T_2\left(x\right)+o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^2\right) f(x)=T2​(x)+o((x−x0​)2)

二次替代

那不如直接待定系数吧!
设:

T2(x)=a0+a1(x−x0)+a(x−x0)2T_2\left(x\right)=a_0+a_1\left(x-x_0\right)+a\left(x-x_0\right)^2 T2​(x)=a0​+a1​(x−x0​)+a(x−x0​)2

小思考: 为什么不把它设为二次函数的一般形式?
那么

f(x)=T2(x)+o((x−x0)2)=a0+a1(x−x0)+a(x−x0)2+o((x−x0)2)f\left(x\right)=T_2\left(x\right)+o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^2\right)=a_0+a_1\left(x-x_0\right)+a\left(x-x_0\right)^2+o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^2\right) f(x)=T2​(x)+o((x−x0​)2)=a0​+a1​(x−x0​)+a(x−x0​)2+o((x−x0​)2)

代入X=X0，即可得到a0:

f(x0)=T2(x0)=a0f\left(x_0\right)=T_2\left(x_0\right)=a_0 f(x0​)=T2​(x0​)=a0​

又有

f(x)−a0−a1(1−x0)−a2(x−x0)2=0((x−x0)2)f\left(x\right)-a_0-a_1\left(1-x_0\right)-a_2\left(x-x_0\right)^2=0\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^2\right) f(x)−a0​−a1​(1−x0​)−a2​(x−x0​)2=0((x−x0​)2)

f(x)−a0x−x0−a1−a2(x−x0)=o((x−x0)2)x−x0\frac{f\left(x\right)-a_0}{x-x_0}-a_1-a_2\left(x-x_0\right)=\frac{o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^2\right)}{x-x_0} x−x0​f(x)−a0​​−a1​−a2​(x−x0​)=x−x0​o((x−x0​)2)​

等式左右同时取极限X->X0即得:

f′(x0)−a1=0f^\prime\left(x_0\right)-a_1=0 f′(x0​)−a1​=0

同理

f(x)−a0−a1(x−x0)(x−x0)2−a2=o((x−x0)2)(x−x0)2\frac{f\left(x\right)-a_0-a_1\left(x-x_0\right)}{\left(x-x_0\right)^2}-a_2=\frac{o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^2\right)}{\left(x-x_0\right)^2} (x−x0​)2f(x)−a0​−a1​(x−x0​)​−a2​=(x−x0​)2o((x−x0​)2)​

取极限X->X0，

f′′(x0)2−a2=0\frac{f^{\prime\prime}\left(x_0\right)}{2}-a_2=0 2f′′(x0​)​−a2​=0

显然，上面求a1和a2的过程并没有在乎f不可导的情况，并且在求极限的过程中用到了L’Hospital’s rule。
最终，可以得到f的二次近似替代:

f(x)=f(x0)+f′(x0)(x−x0)+f′′(x0)2(x−x0)2f\left(x\right)=f\left(x_0\right)+f^\prime\left(x_0\right)\left(x-x_0\right)+\frac{f^{\prime\prime}\left(x_0\right)}{2}\left(x-x_0\right)^2 f(x)=f(x0​)+f′(x0​)(x−x0​)+2f′′(x0​)​(x−x0​)2

推广到用n次函数来近似替代

既然存在二次近似替代，那就大胆假设(三次，四次，乃至于n次都可以替代f)小心求证(直接证明n次吧)(或许可以试试数学归纳法?)。
假设f(x)在x0处n阶可导，设:

f(x)=a0+a1(x−x0)+a2(x−x0)2+…+an(x−x0)n+o((x−x0)n)f\left(x\right)=a_0+a_1\left(x-x_0\right)+a_2\left(x-x_0\right)^2+\ldots+a_n\left(x-x_0\right)^n+o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^n\right) f(x)=a0​+a1​(x−x0​)+a2​(x−x0​)2+…+an​(x−x0​)n+o((x−x0​)n)

f(x0)=a0f\left(x_0\right)=a_0 f(x0​)=a0​

假设n=k时, 满足:

ak=f(k)(x0)k!a_k=\frac{f^{\left(k\right)}\left(x_0\right)}{k!} ak​=k!f(k)(x0​)​

则当n=k+1时:

f(x)−[a0+a1(x−x0)+…+ak(x−x0)k](x−x0)k−ak+1−[ak+2(x−x0)+⋯+an(x−x0)n−k−1]=o((x−x0)n)(x−x0)k\frac{f\left(x\right)-\left[a_0+a_1\left(x-x_0\right)+\ldots+a_k\left(x-x_0\right)^k\right]}{\left(x-x_0\right)^k}-a_{k+1}-\left[a_{k+2}\left(x-x_0\right)+\cdots+a_n\left(x-x_0\right)^{n-k-1}\right]=\frac{o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^n\right)}{\left(x-x_0\right)^k} (x−x0​)kf(x)−[a0​+a1​(x−x0​)+…+ak​(x−x0​)k]​−ak+1​−[ak+2​(x−x0​)+⋯+an​(x−x0​)n−k−1]=(x−x0​)ko((x−x0​)n)​

取极限并且用L’Hospital’s rule即得:

f(k+1)(x0)(k+1)!−ak+1=0\frac{f^{\left(k+1\right)}\left(x_0\right)}{\left(k+1\right)!}-a_{k+1}=0 (k+1)!f(k+1)(x0​)​−ak+1​=0

即假设在n=k+1的时候也成立。由数学归纳法就可以得出:

f(x)=Tn(x)+o((x−x0)n)=∑k=0nakk!(x−x0)k+o((x−x0)n)f\left(x\right)=T_n\left(x\right)+o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^n\right)=\sum_{k=0}^{n}{\frac{a_k}{k!}\left(x-x_0\right)^k}+o\left(\left(x-x_0\right)^n\right) f(x)=Tn​(x)+o((x−x0​)n)=k=0∑n​k!ak​​(x−x0​)k+o((x−x0​)n)

至此，我们完成了从1到n的飞跃。
特殊的，如果X0=0，此时即为Maclaurin展开式:

f(x)=f(0)+f′(0)x+f′′(0)2x2+…+f(n)(0)n!xn+0(xn)f\left(x\right)=f\left(0\right)+f^\prime\left(0\right)x+\frac{f^{\prime\prime}\left(0\right)}{2}x^2+\ldots+\frac{f^{\left(n\right)}\left(0\right)}{n!}x^n+0\left(x^n\right) f(x)=f(0)+f′(0)x+2f′′(0)​x2+…+n!f(n)(0)​xn+0(xn)
